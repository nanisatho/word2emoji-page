## 2. Description of Service

Emojify Text is a mobile application that:

- Converts text input into emoji representations (up to 1,000 characters per input)
- Uses artificial intelligence (specifically Google Gemini AI gemini-2.0-flash-lite model) to generate emoji suggestions
